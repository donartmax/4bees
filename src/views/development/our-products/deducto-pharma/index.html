Deducto Pharma 365

IMPROVE YOUR PHARMACY SUPPLY MANAGEMENT

This product is designed for pharmacy businesses, which mostly have more than 2 suppliers of medicines and pharmaceutical supplements.

In the world of pharmaceutical suppliers, the conditions have become mostly tied with the cash flow you can provide them, so the discount has transfered to cash discount.

In order to track all the cash discounts for more than 10.000 products, you would have to have a special employee just for supply management.

How does it work?

  • Install Deducto Pharma 365.
  • You have a list of your agreed conditions in an Excel file, copy it to the folder named Conditions.
  • Open app, click New order.
  • Enter the name of the product you are searching for. Click Search.
  • The software will now show you all the products with that name, as well as the cash discount % agreed with the supplier.
  • Click add to list, and it will be added to the list with the best conditions
  • Repeat for as many products as you like.
  • When finished click Save, and your ordering list has been saved.
  • If, by any chance, you made a mistake, you can delete any of the products, or clear the whole list.
