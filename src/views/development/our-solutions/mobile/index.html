  • UI, UX and web design

    A high quality design is essential to introduce you to the world, improve your marketing efforts and help sell your idea to potential investors. Our UI/UX and Web design team produce beautiful products that are attractive, functional and intuitive for the end-user. There are many types of user interfaces, but we will consider only those associated with the web:

    Web interface (WUI). This is a set of tools, with the help of which users interact with a web site, or any other application via a browser. New implementations use JavaScript, Ruby, Ruby-on-Rails, and similar technologies. It is possible to perform an online control with their help and it excludes the necessity in upgrading, the HTML-browsers are based on.

    Administrative web interfaces. They are used to work with the servers and remote computers. These interfaces are often referred to as the control panel.

    UI design development helps to create the interface that provides the best, the simplest, and the nicest way for the user’s interaction with the product. Creation of the UI design is essentially a craft, because you create something beautiful and very specific, using the skills, knowledge, and techniques based on your experience.

    UI design makes the interfaces to be beautiful. This includes selection of the color scheme and design that will be a fine-featured and easy to use. However, the choice of color, design, and interaction are not based on personal preferences of the designer, but on the well-defined person-specific reasons, developed by the UX designers (User experience design). With their help, the UI designers realize the visual hierarchy, which will serve as a guide for the users, explaining them what and when to do to achieve their goal.

    UI design creates emotional bonds. After you have configured the basic usability, your users’ loyalty will depend on the uniqueness of your interface. Bright design can attract people to your website, they can stay there if there is something interesting to do. People will forgive your faults; they will follow you and sing praises to you if you can provide them with positive emotions. This is what the UI designer helps to achieve.
